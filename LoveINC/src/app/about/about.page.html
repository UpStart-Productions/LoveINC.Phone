Our Mission & Vision

Mission

We mobilize local churches to transform lives and communities In the Name of Christ.

Vision

We envision a world where Christian churches are united in purpose and fully engaged in actively living out their faith by lovingly serving people in need in their communities.

 How We Serve

We help churches help people by developing a network of local churches and connecting them with opportunities to serve their neighbors in need.

Holistic Care

Addressing spiritual, mental, emotional, and physical needs

Church Collaboration

Each church leans into its strengths, working together

Gap Ministries

Filling unmet needs in the community through partner churches

Transformational Ministry

Educational classes, mentorship, and community events

 Better Together

In every community, people possess gifts and talents that can be used for ministry, but they often don't know how to connect with neighbors in need who are seeking those exact skills at that exact time.

Love INC helps churches mobilize their members to reach out to their neighbors in need, by creating specific, manageable opportunities that utilize the unique skills and talents of their congregation to fill unmet needs in the community.
